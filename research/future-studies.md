## Confidence Levels

| Level | Probability | Description |
|-------|-------------|-------------|
| High | 90%+ | Based on existing prototypes |
| Medium | 70-89% | Strong research evidence |
| Low | 50-69% | Theoretical possibility |

## Data Sources

### Primary Sources
- Peer-reviewed research papers
- Patent filings and grants
- Technology demonstration videos
- Expert interviews and surveys

### Secondary Sources  
- Industry reports and analysis
- Government technology roadmaps
- Academic conference proceedings
- Corporate R&D announcements

## Verification Process

1. **Source Validation** - Check credibility
2. **Multiple Corroboration** - Find supporting evidence
3. **Expert Review** - Domain specialist feedback
4. **Timeline Adjustment** - Update based on new data

## Uncertainty Management

- Provide confidence intervals
- List assumptions explicitly
- Note potential disruptors
- Update predictions regularly

## Continuous Improvement

- Monitor real-world developments
- Compare predictions vs actuals
- Refine methodology based on accuracy
- Incorporate new research techniques
